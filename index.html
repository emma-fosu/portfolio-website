CoSSMic Household Electricity Consumption Analysis

A dashboard to uncover patterns in household electricity consumption and provide actionable recommendations for sustainable energy use.

  • Solar generation is insufficient in winter but exceeds demand by 46% from summer to mid-autumn.
  • Electricity consumption drops by 66% from summer to autumn after rising by 75% during winter.
  • Heat pumps account for only a small share in summer and autumn but consume 67-72% of total electricity in winter.
